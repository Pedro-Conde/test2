Han-Taro Kim

How it came to life

Just like Momoka, I made Kim all the way back in 2019. I was a huge fan of My Hero Academia at the time, so I wanted to create a character with green hair like Midoriya.

Although his initial design was based on Midoriya, I made Kim's personality completely different. He is the 'golden retriever' of the group, always full of energy and playing around. His favorite color is green.

Kim's initial concepts were made all the way back in 2016, but I did not make him an actual character back then, he didn't even have a name. Only in 2019 I decided to take these sketches from when I was 10 and turn it into an OC, even though I didn't have the same proficiency I have nowadays.

Inspirations

Kim fell in the same category as Momoka. His design would change everytime I decided to draw him.

Kim's current design is based on Ezreal from League of Legends, more specifically the Heartsteel skin, and the K-pop artist Hyunjin from Stray Kids.
